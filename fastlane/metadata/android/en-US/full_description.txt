MuSicX is a Material 3 YouTube Music client — a maintained fork of Metrolist with Spotify login, SponsorBlock, Shazam-style music recognition, podcasts, karaoke-style lyrics, and experimental FLAC / Hi-Res streaming via Qobuz.

Playback
• Stream any song or video from YouTube Music
• Background playback
• Download & cache songs for offline listening
• Skip silence, tempo & pitch control, equalizer
• Sleep timer
• SponsorBlock — auto-skip sponsor / intro / outro segments in videos
• Qobuz FLAC / Hi-Res streaming (experimental) — CD-quality 16-bit / 44.1 kHz up to Hi-Res 24-bit / 192 kHz, silent fallback to YouTube on failure
• Continue Listening hero card on Home for one-tap resume
• Audio normalization

Discovery & Lyrics
• Personalized quick picks and Home recommendations
• Local Recently Played row on Home
• LyricsPlus / Experimental Lyrics — word-timed karaoke-style synced lyrics
• AI-powered lyrics translation
• Music Recognition (Shazam-style) — home widget + Quick Settings tile
• Search songs, albums, artists, videos, playlists, podcasts and episodes

Library & Account
• Full library management + local playlists + playlist import
• Reorder songs in playlist or queue
• YouTube Music account login
• Spotify login — sync liked songs, playlists, top tracks and recently played
• Podcasts library — subscribed shows + saved episodes with two tabs

Social
• Listen Together — real-time synced playback with friends

Interface
• Material 3 with Light / Dark / Black / Dynamic themes
• Dynamic color + 19 preset color palettes
• Home screen widget

Reliability
• ANR Watchdog auto-detects UI freezes (≥ 5 s)
• Crash reporter opens sanitized crash reports to GitHub Issues
• Automated nightly sync with upstream Metrolist

MuSicX is community-driven and 100% free — no ads, no trackers, no accounts required. Some integrations (Qobuz resolvers) are community-run third-party services and marked experimental; playback automatically falls back to YouTube when a resolver is offline or a track isn't matched.

Requirements
• Android 6.0 or higher
• YouTube Music available in your region (a VPN or proxy may be needed otherwise)

Source & issue tracker: https://github.com/ufoptg/MuSicX
